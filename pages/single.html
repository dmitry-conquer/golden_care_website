Home Care Services in Naples, FL for Veterans:

Specialized Support with Honor

Our veterans do so much for our country. Many of them become disabled in service. They deserve dignified home care services in Naples. Golden Care honors our veterans by providing them with the best home care. We ensure we repay their service by helping them achieve a high quality of life.

Common Disabilities Among Veteransp

Veterans often suffer disabilities that can make it difficult for them to function, leading to a need for home care services in Naples. Common disabilities include:

  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder: Soldiers who are traumatized in service may continue to relive unpleasant incidents through flashbacks and nightmares. For some, the condition can be debilitating. A caregiver can help by ensuring a safe and supportive home environment.
  • Hearing Loss: Veterans often suffer hearing loss due to exposure to gunshots and explosions. While this condition does not necessarily require home care, a skilled caregiver may be needed if hearing loss is combined with other disabilities. The aide will know how to communicate effectively.
  • Physical Disabilities: Individuals in service may become physically injured and suffer from amputations, strains, and nerve damage, making it difficult to perform daily tasks. A home aide offers various services that ensure a safe and hygienic environment for former military members.
  • Chronic Health Conditions: Chronic health conditions, like heart disease, diabetes, and cancer, are common among veterans due to deployment-related exposures, socioeconomic factors, and lifestyle choices. A home aide can oversee basic needs, medication management, and essential healthcare.
