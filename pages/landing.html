Private Pay for Home Care

Private Pay offers families the flexibility to get premium, personalized home care services without the restrictions of government-funded programs. At Golden Care, we specialize in working with private pay clients, ensuring you or your loved one receives the highest level of care from licensed, bonded, and insured caregivers.

Why Choose Golden Care for Private Pay?

  1. Exclusive focus on quality care.
  2. Trained, vetted caregivers who are W-2 employees (not contractors).
  3. Services tailored to individual needs.

Long-Term Care Insurance (LTCI)

Simplifying Your Care Journey with Long-Term Care Insurance

At Golden Care, we know that navigating Long-Term Care Insurance (LTCI) can feel overwhelming. That’s why we’re here to make the process simple and stress-free.

What is LTCI?

Long-Term Care Insurance is a policy that helps cover the cost of non-medical home care services, such as personal care, companionship, and homemaker services.

How We Help:

  • We assist with understanding your policy benefits.
  • We handle the paperwork and claims process.
  • We ensure you're maximizing your coverage for the care you need.

Let us focus on the details so you can focus on what matters
most—your loved one’s comfort and well-being.

Home Care for Heroes

At Golden Care, we honor our heroes by providing specialized care programs for veterans.

Veterans Benefits May Cover Home Care Services:

  1. Assistance with daily living (bathing, dressing, grooming).
  2. Homemaker and companionship care.
  3. Transitional care after hospital discharge.

We are approved by the VA and recognized as representatives to provide home care benefits covered by the VA for those who qualify.
