About Golden Care

Golden Care is a trusted home care agency in Naples, FL, providing compassionate non-medical support for seniors. We help clients live safely and comfortably at home with personalized care tailored to their needs. Our reliable caregivers enhance daily life while promoting independence, dignity, and peace of mind — always with the gold standard of quality.

Who We Are –
The Golden Care

Golden Care's primary mission is to provide genuine care and comfort for our clients. We never compromise on our core values: respect, compassion, and excellence. We treat every senior as a member of our family, committing to providing the best level of care tailored to each individual. Our “golden rule” is simple: care for your loved ones as if they were your own. This means listening to their needs, respecting their wishes, and doing everything possible to meet even the unspoken needs. When you choose Golden Care for home care in Naples, you get a dedicated partner who puts your loved one’s well-being first, every step of the way.

What makes Golden Care stand out?

We combine a concierge-level of service with a personal touch. Our approach to care is holistic, addressing not just physical assistance but also emotional support and social engagement. From the moment you reach out to us, you’ll find a warm, knowledgeable team ready to guide you. We are fully licensed, bonded, and insured, giving your family peace of mind. Most of all, we are passionate local professionals driven by a desire to help seniors in our community thrive at home.

Paying for In-Home Care

At Golden Care, we understand that figuring out how to pay for care can feel
overwhelming. That’s why we offer clear guidance and flexible options to help families
access in-home care in Naples and neighboring areas with confidence and ease.

Private Pay

Many families choose to pay for services directly through private pay. This option offers the most flexibility, allowing you to select the exact type and frequency of care your loved one needs, with no long-term contracts or deposits — services are billed weekly after care is provided. We work with you to create a personalized care plan that fits both your needs and your budget.

Veterans Benefits

For those who have served, we proudly help veterans and their spouses take advantage of available benefits. Programs like the VA Aid & Attendance Pension can help cover the cost of home care. Our experienced team can guide you through:

  • Determining eligibility
  • Completing the required VA paperwork
  • Coordinating care in line with benefit requirements
