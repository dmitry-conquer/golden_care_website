The Golden Care

Golden Care's primary mission is to provide genuine care and comfort for our clients. We never compromise on our core values: respect, compassion, and excellence. We treat every senior as a member of our family, committing to providing the best level of care tailored to each individual. Our “golden rule” is simple: care for your loved ones as if they were your own. This means listening to their needs, respecting their wishes, and doing everything possible to meet even the unspoken needs. When you choose Golden Care for home care in Naples, you get a dedicated partner who puts your loved one’s well-being first, every step of the way.

Paying for In-Home Care

At Golden Care, we understand that figuring out how to pay for care can feel overwhelming. That’s why we offer clear guidance and flexible options to help families access in-home care in Naples and neighboring areas with confidence and ease.

Private Pay

Many families choose to pay for services directly through private pay. This option offers the most flexibility, allowing you to select the exact type and frequency of care your loved one needs, with no long-term contracts or deposits — services are billed weekly after care is provided. We work with you to create a personalized care plan that fits both your needs and your budget.
