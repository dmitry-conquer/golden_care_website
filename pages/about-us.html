Why Home Health Care?

For people in search of personal care or companionship for a loved one, home care is the best way for many patients to preserve their independence and remain in the comfort of their own homes. Whether you are an older adult who suffers from a chronic condition or even just transitioning home from a hospital visit, home care can be a viable and affordable option for you or your loved ones

Virtually every personal care or aide service that a hospital, assisted living facility or nursing home may offer can now be provided in the comfort and privacy of your home. Because of this, more and more people are looking to spend their resources for care at home versus moving into a facility.

Aging in Place: 87 percent of adults age 65+ want to stay in their current home and community as they age.

— AARP

With home care your loved one can be guaranteed one-on-one attention versus the high patient-to-aide ratio offered at nursing homes, assisted living facilities or hospitals. If a family member cannot be left alone, Golden Care has around-the-clock Certified Nursing Assistants and Home Health Aides to make sure they are safe and well cared for 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
